About Eleanote

Eleanote was created by Doug Ridyard, a practicing urologist in New Hampshire, out of a desire to work more efficiently in his clinic. He tried several ambient scribe platforms but found that none of them fit the way he actually practiced — each required him to alter his workflow around the AI instead of the AI working for him.

He built Eleanote to do the opposite: to harness the power of AI to fit the needs of the provider.

Eleanote is currently in a testing phase. Once it has been refined and is ready for a broader base of users, we plan to transition it into a commercial product. If you find Eleanote useful, please share it with your friends and colleagues.
