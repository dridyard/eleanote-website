Business Associate Agreement

Draft · In preparation with HIPAA counsel

The Business Associate Agreement (BAA) sets out the HIPAA-specific obligations between you (the covered entity) and Eleanote LLC (the business associate) for any Protected Health Information (PHI) handled in connection with the Service. It is one of the three documents that govern your use of Eleanote, alongside the Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy.
