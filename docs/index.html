Capabilities

Built around real clinic friction.

These are the areas Eleanote is being developed around. The wording is intentionally plain because the product should be judged by how it works.

Clinical note drafting

Turn visit context into a draft that can be reviewed, corrected, and placed into the physician's workflow.

Physician preferences

Support the structure, phrasing, and level of detail a clinician actually wants in their documentation.

EMR workflow support

Reduce repetitive work around orders, follow-up details, indications, and other administrative steps.

Review-first design

Keep the clinician in control with output that is intended to be reviewed before it is used clinically.

Simple interface

Prioritize the few screens clinicians need instead of a busy dashboard full of unused features.

Development with feedback

Shape the product around practical physician input rather than broad marketing assumptions.

Positioning

A serious tool, kept simple.

Eleanote is not presented here as a finished enterprise platform or a magic replacement for clinical judgment. It is a developing software product focused on making documentation and EMR-adjacent work less cumbersome for physicians.

  • No inflated time-savings promises.
  • No generic AI hype language.
  • Clear product explanation for people who already heard about it directly.
  • Designed to add real screenshots and short demos as the product matures.
