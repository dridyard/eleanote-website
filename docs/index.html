An ambient AI scribe
built by a doctor, for doctors.

eleanote is a program installed on your clinic laptop. What it does:

  • Precharting in seconds.
  • Notes written with frontier-quality AI.
  • Personalize your notes exactly the way you want them.
  • Immediate feedback can be applied to any note. Eleanote learns your preferences quickly.
  • Transfer diagnosis codes, orders, and other programmable inputs into your EMR with the push of a button.
  • Real-time order capture. Eleanote can be programmed to listen during a visit and stage orders so they're entered before the visit ends.
